Cite Us

Please reference HPCC in any research report, journal, or publication that requires citation of authors' work. Recognition of the HPCC resources you used to perform research is important for acquiring funding for the next generation of hardware, support services, and our research and development activities in HPC, visualization, data storage, and grid infrastructure.

At minimum, a citation should include: High Performance Computing Center (HPCC) Texas Tech University
